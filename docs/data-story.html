Navigating Through Murky Waters

Before we venture further, let’s address the elephant in the room—our major limitation. As much as we’d like to believe that our data analytics wand can reveal all secrets, the truth is more complicated.

It’s incredibly challenging to draw a direct correlation between the musings of an influencer—no matter how influential—and the ebb and flow of a cryptocurrency’s value. Did Elon’s tweets really send Dogecoin to the moon, or are we just seeing patterns in the stars?
